Case Study

PRE-FLIGHT to AIRBORNE

RockaFairy is a fairly new public benefit non-profit that provides Southern Oregon free access for needy families to a vast instrument inventory, recording studio, private rehearsal space, and public event venue. Their goal is to grow community outreach by offering music instruction and a check-out process for those needing instruments. My goal was to enhance the user’s experience and enhance KPIs.

User Research

Research began with a tour of their facility, RockaFairy Hall, & a stakeholder interview with the founder Shane Ross. He revealed that a large amount of homeless individuals come into the Hall every day as a result of a huge surge in the homeless population -- a lot of them due to a series of sweeping fires in the area. Visitors & members also include parents, who can’t afford to expand their children’s music training because of the expense of instruments and instruction. After user testing of the original site & interviews I developed out a user persona who inhabited all of those aspects.

“At least 4-6 homeless come through our doors every day. If we could get upwards of just 400 members (the Hall) could be a completely self-sustainable non-profit.” - Shane Ross
